Our Story

EventBox was created with a mission to connect students to the most exciting and enriching college events. Whether you're interested in tech talks, music festivals, or charity runs, we have it all! Our platform makes it easy for students to discover, join, and organize events that will help them grow and have fun. EventBox is here to ensure every college event becomes a memorable experience.
